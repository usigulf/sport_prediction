Frequently asked questions

Which sports does octobetiQ cover?

We cover eight leagues — six soccer competitions (Premier League, Champions League, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, MLS) plus NFL and NBA. Coverage depth varies by competition depending on licensed data and sync jobs.

How do predictions work?

Machine learning models trained on historical game data estimate win probabilities. Each game shows home and away win probabilities and a confidence level (low, medium, high). Soccer uses three-way (1X2) style probabilities where applicable.

What is the daily prediction limit?

Free accounts can view a limited number of predictions per day. Upgrade to Premium in Profile → Subscription for unlimited predictions and full explanations.

What do pick strength stars mean?

Each pick has a 1–5 star strength from model confidence: 5 = high, 3 = medium, 1 = low. Many users focus on 3-star picks and above.

What are high-confidence picks?

When the model is very confident, we mark the prediction as high confidence. Enable push notifications under Settings → Push notifications if you want alerts.

How is model accuracy calculated?

For finished games we compare the latest stored prediction to the final score. Soccer uses home / draw / away outcomes; other sports use the predicted favorite vs the winner. Open Model accuracy in the app for methodology, a 30-day rollup, and coverage notes. Numbers are informational, not betting advice.

How do I add favorite teams?

Open a game from Home or Games, then tap the star next to a team name. Your Favorites tab shows games for those teams.
