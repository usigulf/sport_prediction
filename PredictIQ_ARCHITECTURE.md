## 1. Product Vision & User Personas

### Vision

PredictIQ is an **information-only** sports intelligence platform that democratizes the
kind of predictive analytics previously available only to professional analysts and
institutional sports desks. We surface transparent, explainable AI predictions — not
gambling advice.

**Legal positioning:** PredictIQ is an analytics and entertainment product. It does not
facilitate, enable, or encourage wagering. All predictions carry explicit disclaimers.

### User Personas

| Persona | Description | Needs | Willingness to Pay |
|---|---|---|---|
| **The Casual Fan** (60%) | Watches 2-3 games/week, checks scores on phone | Quick predictions, easy UI, social sharing, "fun factor" | Free tier, occasional IAP |
| **The Analyst** (25%) | Fantasy league player, reads advanced stats | Deep model explanations, historical accuracy, custom alerts, player-level predictions | Premium subscriber ($9.99/mo) |
| **The Content Creator** (10%) | Sports podcaster, blogger, YouTuber | Embeddable widgets, API access, trend data for content | Pro tier ($29.99/mo) or API tier |
| **The Enterprise Client** (5%) | Media companies, sports networks, data providers | White-label API, bulk predictions, SLA guarantees | B2B contract ($5K-50K/mo) |

### Supported Sports (Phased)

- **Phase 1 (Launch):** NFL, NBA, Premier League (soccer), MLB
- **Phase 2 (Month 6):** NHL, La Liga, Serie A, Bundesliga, UFC/MMA
- **Phase 3 (Month 12):** Tennis (ATP/WTA), Cricket (IPL/International), College Football/Basketball
- **Phase 4 (Month 18):** Esports (LoL, CS2, Dota 2), F1, Rugby
